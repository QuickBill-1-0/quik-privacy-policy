At Quik Payment Limited ("Quik," "we," "our," or "us"), we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. By accessing or using the Services, you agree to this Privacy Policy and consent to the collection, use, and sharing of your information as described herein.

1. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide you with better services, including:

1.1 Personal Information

When you register for a Quik account, you provide us with personal information such as:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Bank Verification Number (BVN)
  • Date of Birth
  • Bank Account Details
  • Fidelity Bank Account Number (assigned upon account creation)

1.2 Transaction Information

We collect information related to your financial transactions on the platform, including:

  • Payment and transfer history
  • Recipient and sender details
  • Transaction amounts
  • Date, time, and location of the transaction

1.3 Device Information

We may collect device-related information, such as:

  • Device model
  • Operating system
  • Unique device identifiers
  • IP address
  • Browser type and settings
  • Mobile carrier

1.4 Location Information

With your permission, we may collect precise geolocation data from your mobile device to provide location-based services like fraud detection or enabling specific features based on your region.

1.5 Cookies and Similar Technologies

We may use cookies, web beacons, and other tracking technologies to collect information about your interactions with our Site and App to enhance your user experience. You can adjust your browser settings to reject cookies if you prefer.

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

2.1 To Provide and Improve Our Services

  • Facilitate account creation and transactions (e.g., transfers, payments, purchases)
  • Automatically assign a Fidelity Bank account number for secure fund storage
  • Process and manage your transactions securely
  • Enhance and improve user experience on the platform
  • Send updates about new features, services, and offers

2.2 To Ensure Security

  • Detect and prevent fraudulent activities and unauthorized transactions
  • Monitor suspicious activity and protect users from unauthorized access
  • Verify your identity for compliance with anti-fraud regulations

2.3 To Communicate with You

  • Send important updates regarding your account, including confirmations, alerts, and notifications
  • Respond to customer service inquiries and provide support
  • Send promotional messages and offers with your consent

2.4 To Comply with Legal Obligations

  • Meet regulatory requirements related to anti-money laundering (AML), Know Your Customer (KYC), and other legal obligations
  • Report to law enforcement as required by law

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure, including:

  • Encryption: Your personal and financial data is encrypted during transmission using secure SSL technology.
  • Access Control: Access to sensitive data is restricted to authorized personnel only.
  • Two-Factor Authentication (2FA): We offer 2FA as an additional layer of security for your account.

However, please note that no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We strive to protect your personal information but cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Your Privacy Rights

You can access, update, or correct your personal information by logging into your account and managing your settings or by contacting our support team.

5.2 Opt-Out of Communications

You can opt-out of receiving promotional emails by following the unsubscribe instructions included in the emails. Please note that you may still receive transactional and important account-related communications.

5.3 Delete Your Account

If you wish to delete your account, contact us at info@payquik.io. Please note that certain information related to your transactions may be retained for legal and regulatory purposes even after your account is closed.
